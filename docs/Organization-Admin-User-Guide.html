4. Managing Team Members

Inviting New Members

  1. Navigate to Organization Dashboard

    Click Organization in sidebar, then select your organization.

  2. Click "Invite Member"

    The invitation dialog will open with a form.

  3. Enter Member Details

    • Email Address: Enter the team member's email
    • Role: Choose from:
      • Admin - Full management capabilities (like you)
      • Member - Access to shared emails and collaboration
      • Viewer - Read-only access to organization data

  4. Send Invitation

    Click Send Invite. The member receives an email invitation with instructions to join.

‚úÖ What Happens Next

The invited member receives a welcome email with login credentials and setup instructions. They appear in "Pending Invites" until they accept.

Managing Existing Members

View All Members

Scroll to the "Team Members" section to see a list of all organization members with their roles and join dates.

Change Member Role

  1. Find the member in the list
  2. Click "Edit Role" button
  3. Select new role from dropdown (Admin, Member, or Viewer)
  4. Click "Update Role"
  5. Confirmation message appears
‚ö†Ô∏è Important

You cannot remove the organization owner or change their role. Only the owner can transfer ownership.

Remove a Member

  1. Find the member in the list
  2. Click the trash icon (üóëÔ∏è)
  3. Confirm removal in the dialog
  4. Member loses access immediately

5. Email Account Management

Connecting Email Accounts

  1. Navigate to Email Accounts

    Go to Settings ‚Üí Email Accounts or click Connect in the sidebar.

  2. Click "Connect Email Account"

    The provider selection dialog appears.

  3. Choose Your Provider

    Select from available options:

    • Gmail - Google Workspace or personal Gmail accounts
    • Outlook - Microsoft 365 or Outlook.com accounts
    • Yahoo Mail - Yahoo email accounts
    • IMAP - Any other email provider with IMAP access

  4. Complete OAuth Flow

    You'll be redirected to your email provider's login page. Sign in and grant the necessary permissions for EaseMail to access your account.

  5. Account Syncs Automatically

    Once connected, your emails will begin syncing immediately. This may take a few minutes for large mailboxes.

Shared Email Accounts

Set up shared email accounts (like support@company.com) for team collaboration:

Benefits of Shared Accounts

  • Team collaboration on support emails
  • Shared email history across all team members
  • Coordinated responses prevent duplicate replies
  • Visibility into team workload

Setting Up Shared Access

  1. Organization owner or admin connects the shared email account
  2. Grant access to specific team members or make it organization-wide
  3. Members can view and respond from the shared inbox
  4. All actions are logged for accountability

Email Account Settings

Set Primary Account

Your primary account is used by default when composing new emails. To change it:

  1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Email Accounts
  2. Click on the account you want to make primary
  3. Click "Set as Primary"

Sync Settings

  • Auto-sync: Real-time synchronization (recommended)
  • Manual sync: Click refresh button to sync
  • Sync frequency: Configure in account settings

Disconnect Account

‚ö†Ô∏è Warning

Disconnecting an account removes it from EaseMail but does not delete any emails from your actual email account.

  1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Email Accounts
  2. Click on the account to expand
  3. Click "Disconnect"
  4. Confirm in dialog

Accessing Analytics

Navigate to Organization ‚Üí Analytics to view comprehensive reports.

Available Reports

Email Activity Dashboard

  • Total Emails: Volume of sent and received emails
  • Response Time: Average time to first response
  • Email by Category: Distribution across folders (inbox, sent, archived)
  • Sender Analysis: Most active senders and receivers

  • Collaboration Metrics: Shared email engagement and teamwork
  • Peak Hours: When your team is most active

Calendar Analytics

  • Meeting Distribution: Types and frequency of meetings
  • Time Allocation: Where time is being spent
  • Attendee Analysis: Meeting participation patterns
  • Conflict Detection: Scheduling conflicts and overlaps

Usage Metrics

  • Feature Usage: Which features are most utilized
  • AI Usage: How often AI-powered features are used
  • API Calls: API integration usage (if applicable)
  • Storage Usage: Email and attachment storage consumption

Exporting Reports

  1. Navigate to Desired Report

    Select the report you want to export from the Analytics dashboard.

  2. Click "Export" Button

    Usually located in the top right corner of the report.

  3. Choose Format

    • CSV - Best for spreadsheets and data analysis
    • PDF - Best for presentations and sharing
    • JSON - Best for integrations and custom processing

  4. Download File

    The file downloads to your computer immediately.

Scheduling Reports

Set up automated reports to be delivered via email:

  1. Go to Analytics ‚Üí Scheduled Reports
  2. Click "New Scheduled Report"
  3. Configure:
    • Report type (email activity, team performance, etc.)
    • Frequency (daily, weekly, monthly)
    • Recipients (email addresses)
    • Format (PDF or CSV)
  4. Click "Schedule"
  5. Reports will be sent automatically via email

8. Best Practices

Team Management Best Practices

‚úÖ DO

  • Regularly review team member access
  • Use appropriate roles (don't make everyone Admin)
  • Remove access for departed members immediately
  • Document role changes internally
  • Set up onboarding process for new members

‚ùå DON'T

  • Share admin credentials with multiple people
  • Leave pending invitations open indefinitely
  • Grant admin access without proper vetting
  • Forget to revoke contractor access after projects

Email Organization Best Practices

‚úÖ DO

  • Use shared accounts for team collaboration
  • Set up email rules for auto-categorization
  • Create templates for common responses
  • Use labels/folders to organize emails
  • Archive old emails regularly

‚ùå DON'T

  • Use personal email for organization business
  • Delete important emails (archive instead)
  • Ignore email security warnings
  • Share sensitive info without encryption

Security Best Practices

üîí Security Checklist

  • Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) whenever possible
  • Review audit logs regularly for suspicious activity
  • Report any security concerns immediately to support
  • Keep your email accounts secure with updated passwords
  • Never share your password with anyone, including support
  • Use different passwords for different services
  • Don't click suspicious links in emails
  • Always lock your computer when stepping away

Communication Best Practices

  • Respond Promptly: Answer team member questions quickly
  • Document Decisions: Keep records of important policy changes
  • Keep Owner Informed: Communicate significant issues up the chain
  • Announce Changes: Let team know about policy or setting changes
  • Provide Feedback: Share suggestions for platform improvements
  • Don't Assume: Ensure everyone knows how to use new features
  • Plan for Downtime: Announce maintenance windows in advance

9. Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions

Can't Log In

Symptoms

"Invalid credentials" error or password not working

Solutions:

  1. Verify you're using the correct email address
  2. Check if Caps Lock is on
  3. Try password reset:
    • Click "Forgot Password" on login page
    • Check email for reset link
    • Create new password
  4. Clear browser cache and cookies
  5. Try a different browser

Email Not Syncing

Symptoms

New emails not appearing, sync status shows "Error"

Solutions:

  1. Click the Refresh button manually
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Verify email account is still connected in Settings ‚Üí Email Accounts
  4. Reconnect email account:
    • Disconnect the account
    • Connect again through OAuth
  5. Check Nylas service status if issue persists

Can't Invite Team Members

Symptoms

"Invite Member" button grayed out or error when sending invitation

Solutions:

  1. Check organization seat limit in Organization dashboard
  2. Verify you have Admin or Owner role
  3. Ensure email address isn't already a member
  4. Verify valid email address format
  5. Contact owner if all seats are used

Calendar Not Loading

Symptoms

Calendar shows blank, 500 error, or "No events" message

Solutions:

  1. Refresh the page
  2. Ensure email account is connected
  3. Check calendar permissions (reconnect email account if needed)
  4. Try connecting to Microsoft Teams calendar
  5. Clear browser cache

10. Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: What's the difference between Admin and Owner?

A: Admins can manage members and settings, but cannot delete the organization, transfer ownership, or modify billing. Only the Owner has these capabilities.

Q: How many email accounts can I connect?

A: You can connect unlimited personal email accounts. Shared organization accounts depend on your subscription plan.

Q: Can I use EaseMail on mobile?

A: Yes! EaseMail is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers. Native mobile apps are coming soon.

Q: Is my email data secure?

A: Yes. We use enterprise-grade encryption, secure OAuth authentication, and never store your email passwords. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Team Management

Q: How do I know if someone has accepted their invitation?

A: Accepted invitations disappear from "Pending Invites" and the user appears in the "Team Members" list.

Q: Can I have multiple Admins?

A: Yes! You can have as many Admins as needed, within your seat limit.

Q: What happens when I remove a team member?

A: They immediately lose access to the organization. Their personal email accounts remain connected to their individual account, but they can't access organization resources.

Q: Can I demote myself from Admin?

A: No. Only the Owner can change your role. Contact your organization owner if you need your role changed.

Email & Calendar

Q: Why aren't my sent emails showing in EaseMail?

A: Check your email account sync settings. Click the refresh button or try reconnecting your account.

Q: Can I schedule emails to send later?

A: Yes! When composing an email, click "Schedule" and choose your desired send time.

Q: How do I set up out-of-office replies?

A: Go to Settings ‚Üí Auto-Reply and configure your message and dates.

Q: Does EaseMail work with Microsoft Teams?

A: Yes! Connect your Microsoft account to sync Teams meetings with your EaseMail calendar.

Billing & Plans

Q: How do I upgrade our plan?

A: Contact the organization owner. Only they can modify billing and subscriptions.

Q: What happens if we exceed our seat limit?

A: You won't be able to invite new members until you upgrade your plan or remove existing members to free up seats.

Q: Can we add seats mid-billing cycle?

A: Yes! Contact your organization owner to add seats. Billing is prorated for the remaining billing period.

Q: Do inactive members count toward the seat limit?

A: Yes. Inactive members still occupy seats. Remove them to free up space for new members.

Technical

Q: Which browsers are supported?

A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions recommended).

Q: Can I use keyboard shortcuts?

A: Yes! Press ? anywhere in the app to see all available keyboard shortcuts.

Q: How often does email sync?

A: Real-time for most actions. Full synchronization occurs every 5 minutes automatically.

Q: Can I export all my data?

A: Yes. Go to Settings ‚Üí Data Export and request a full data export. You'll receive a download link via email.
